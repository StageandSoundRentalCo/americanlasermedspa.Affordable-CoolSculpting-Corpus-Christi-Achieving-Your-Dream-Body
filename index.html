What is CoolSculpting?

CoolSculpting is a non-invasive body contouring procedure that targets and freezes fat cells, helping to reduce stubborn fat in specific areas of the body. The treatment is FDA-approved and has been proven to provide lasting, noticeable results.

It’s ideal for individuals who are already maintaining a healthy lifestyle but need assistance in eliminating small pockets of fat that refuse to budge.

CoolSculpting works by using a technology called cryolipolysis, which involves controlled cooling to target fat cells beneath the skin. These fat cells are frozen, causing them to die over time.

Once they are gone, the body naturally processes and eliminates them, leaving you with a more sculpted appearance.

How the CoolSculpting Treatment Works

If you’re considering CoolSculpting, you might be wondering what the process entails. The treatment is quick, easy, and virtually painless. Here’s what you can expect during your CoolSculpting session:

1. Consultation

Your CoolSculpting journey begins with a consultation at this leading Corpus Christi aesthetic center. During this consultation, the provider will assess your body, discuss your goals, and help determine if CoolSculpting is right for you.

This is a great time to ask any questions you may have and learn more about the procedure.

2. Treatment Preparation

Once your treatment plan is in place, the area to be treated will be marked, and a gel pad will be applied to protect the skin. The CoolSculpting applicator will then be placed on the targeted area, and the cooling process will begin.

3. Fat Freezing

The CoolSculpting machine will use controlled cooling to freeze the fat cells in the treated area. This process takes about 35-60 minutes per area, depending on the size and location of the treatment.

You may feel a cold sensation at first, but it typically subsides after a few minutes. Many patients find the procedure comfortable enough to relax, read, or even nap during the treatment.

4. Post-Treatment

After the treatment is complete, the applicator is removed, and the area may be massaged to help break down the frozen fat cells. You may experience some redness, swelling, or numbness in the treated area, but these side effects are usually temporary and subside within a few hours or days.

5. Results

The results of CoolSculpting are not immediate, as it takes time for the body to eliminate the frozen fat cells. You can start to notice visible changes in the treated area within 3-4 weeks, with the most significant results appearing after about 2-3 months. With continued healthy habits, the results can be permanent.
